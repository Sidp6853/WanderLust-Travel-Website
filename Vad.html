About

Vadodara

Vadodara, the magnificent city of Gujarat is also known as Baroda. It is located on the Vishvamitri River banks. It is a densely populated city of the state. It is a home some of the best universities in India. This was earlier a state which changed to city gradually. Vadodara is an acclaimed destination for tourists inviting a huge number of people to visit the glorious city. It poses a rich cultural and historical heritage. The city is known as Sanskari Nagari due to rich cultural traditions.
